How the Talent Score is calculated

The Talent Score is a weighted index that surfaces standout performers across the season:

  • Times in the Best (40%) – consistency of impact
  • Goals scored (25%) – attacking output
  • Games played (15%) – availability & reliability
  • Recent form (20%) – performance over last 3 games vs season average

Scores are normalised 0–100 within the current grade filter so players compare fairly.
