Talent Score blends four PlayHQ data points into a single weighted number, then scales it so consistent contributors rise above one-week wonders.

InputWeightWhy it matters
BOG votes (1–6 per game)× 8Coach-judged impact — the strongest available signal of match influence.
BOG firsts (named #1 best)+ 6 bonusRewards game-winning performances, not just consistent voting.
Goals× 5Scoreboard impact — particularly weighted for forwards.
Wins× 2Small contribution to recognise team success.

Total points are divided by √games rather than games, so a player with 6 votes from 6 games outranks 6 votes from 1 game. A score of 25+ is elite at VAFA Women's level; 40+ is competition-defining.

Dashboard Top 5 lists also require a minimum of 3 games played to filter out small-sample noise.
