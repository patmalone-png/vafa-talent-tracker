📊 How the Talent Score is calculated ▾

The Talent Score is a weighted index that surfaces standout performers across the season:

  • Times in the Best (40%) – consistency of impact
  • Goals scored (25%) – attacking output
  • Games played (15%) – availability & reliability
  • Recent form (20%) – performance over last 3 games vs season average

Scores are normalised 0–100 within the current grade filter so players compare fairly.

🔥 How the Form Indicator is calculated ▾

The Form Indicator (0–100) measures how a player has performed in their last 3 games compared to their season average.

  • Best player appearances – each "in the best" finish counts as 1 impact point
  • Goals kicked – each goal counts as 0.5 impact points

The final score blends Trend (50%) recent ÷ season impact, and Volume (50%) raw recent impact.

50 = season average · 75+ = trending up · under 30 = trending down.
